Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Home Cyber Audit?

A practical, friendly safety review of your home Wi-Fi, devices, privacy and online behaviours — designed for real families.

Do I need to be technical?

No. Everything is explained clearly with simple steps anyone can follow.

How long does it take?

Most family audits take between 45–60 minutes.

Do you help with children’s online safety?

Yes — we cover gaming, Roblox, TikTok, device controls, privacy settings and hidden risks.

Is this like antivirus?

No — we take a full “household defence” approach that covers hardware, behaviour, accounts and risks.
