Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your Digital MOT

General Questions

What is the Digital MOT?

It’s a 45–60 minute online session where we assess your home’s cyber-safety, check key risks, and fix issues live during the call.

Who is Cyber Seeds for?

Families, parents, grandparents, and households wanting clearer guidance on staying safe online.

Do I need technical knowledge?

No! We explain everything clearly and simply — no jargon.

During the Session

How does the session take place?

We use Zoom or Google Meet. You’ll receive a link after payment.

What do you check?

  • ✔ WiFi & router security
  • ✔ Device hygiene & updates
  • ✔ Password & login safety
  • ✔ Exposure scan & leak check
  • ✔ Scam & fraud awareness
  • ✔ Child online safety review

Do you fix issues during the call?

Yes — most fixes happen live during the session.

After the Session

Do I get a report?

Yes, you receive a personalised Family Safety Plan summarising findings and improvements.

Can I ask questions afterwards?

Absolutely. You can reach out anytime through the contact page.
