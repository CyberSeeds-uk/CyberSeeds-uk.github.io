FAQ

Answers to the most common questions.

What is the Cyber Seeds audit?

A simple 45–60 minute home digital-safety MOT designed for families.

Do you need access to my devices?

No — we guide you, and you stay fully in control.

Is this suitable for children?

Yes! We help parents improve digital wellbeing across the whole household.

Do you offer in-person sessions?

Yes, in selected Greater Manchester areas.

Do you offer remote sessions?

Yes — we serve families anywhere in the UK.
