Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Cyber Seeds and our family cyber-safety audits.

About the Service

What is a Cyber Seeds 5-Point Audit?

The 5-Point Audit is a calm, friendly digital safety check designed for families. We review WiFi security, device hygiene, privacy exposure, scam risks and child digital wellbeing.

How long does it take?

Most sessions last 45–60 minutes depending on the number of devices.

Is this service remote?

Yes — the entire audit is delivered remotely via video session for safety and simplicity.

Payment & Scheduling

How do I pay?

You can pay securely using Stripe on our pricing page. After payment, you’ll be automatically redirected to schedule your session.

Do I need to book before paying?

No — payment comes first. After payment, you’ll be taken directly to the scheduling calendar.

Can I reschedule?

Yes, you can reschedule through your Calendly confirmation email.

Safety & Privacy

Do you need access to my devices?

No. We guide you through everything step-by-step. You stay in complete control of your devices.

What personal data do you store?

Only what is provided voluntarily during the session. We do not access or store passwords, photos, or private content.

Is the session suitable for children?

Yes. We support families with children of all ages and provide tailored digital wellbeing guidance.
