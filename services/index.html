Our Services

Simple, friendly digital-safety support designed for real UK families.

The Digital MOT (45–60 minutes)

The Digital MOT is our most popular service — a calm, guided check of your home’s digital safety. No tech jargon. No overwhelm. Just clear, practical support.

  • ✔ WiFi & router security review
  • ✔ Device safety check (phones, tablets, laptops, consoles)
  • ✔ Privacy settings & exposure check
  • ✔ Email/data leak scan
  • ✔ Scam & fraud protection guidance
  • ✔ Children’s digital-wellbeing support

You’ll finish with a clear list of improvements you can action the same day — plus ongoing advice if you need it.

WiFi & Router Safety Check

Your WiFi is the front door to your digital home. If it’s left on old defaults or weak passwords, anyone nearby — or even bots online — can try to access it.

We check:

  • ✔ Password strength & hidden risks
  • ✔ Security mode (WPA3 / WPA2)
  • ✔ Router firmware & setup
  • ✔ Guest network configuration
  • ✔ Smart devices on your network

We show you exactly what’s safe, what needs updating, and how to protect your digital home properly.

Device Security Review

Every device your family uses — phones, tablets, consoles, laptops — plays a role in your household’s safety.

We look for:

  • ✔ Outdated apps or software
  • ✔ Unsafe settings
  • ✔ Permissions & privacy leaks
  • ✔ Console chat, friend requests & online play risks
  • ✔ Security features that may not be switched on

You’ll get clear, step-by-step recommendations to strengthen every device in your home.

Email & Password Leak Scan

You may not realise it, but many people’s email addresses and passwords have already been leaked online through old breaches.

We check:

  • ✔ Exposure in known breaches
  • ✔ If passwords are weak or reused
  • ✔ Whether scammers can access private data

If you’ve been exposed, we show you exactly what to fix — quickly and safely.

Scam & Fraud Prevention

Scams targeting UK families are evolving weekly — fake parcel texts, bank impersonation calls, WhatsApp “mum/dad” scams, subscription traps, gaming scams, and more.

We help you recognise:

  • ✔ Red flags in messages and calls
  • ✔ Fake websites & login pages
  • ✔ Phishing emails
  • ✔ Social-media impersonation risks

You’ll learn exactly how to protect yourself — with simple, memorable tips that actually work.

Children’s Digital Wellbeing

Children don’t need strict rules — they need awareness, confidence and gentle guidance. We support parents with practical, age-appropriate advice.

We cover:

  • ✔ Safer settings for popular apps
  • ✔ Healthy screen-time habits
  • ✔ Online friendships & gaming chat
  • ✔ Handling online pressure & trends
  • ✔ Building resilience without fear

Our approach is human, kind, and grounded in real-world experience — not scare tactics.
