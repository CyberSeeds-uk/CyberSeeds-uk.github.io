Digital Safety Audit (The Digital MOT)

The Cyber Seeds Digital MOT is a relaxed and friendly 45–60 minute online session where we check your home’s cyber-safety step-by-step.

You’ll get a complete overview of the risks in your home, plus calm, practical fixes along the way.

  • ✔ WiFi & router security check
  • ✔ Device hygiene review
  • ✔ Exposure scan (password leaks & privacy risks)
  • ✔ Scam & fraud protection coaching
  • ✔ Child digital wellbeing review
  • ✔ Live fixes during the session
  • ✔ Personalised family safety plan
