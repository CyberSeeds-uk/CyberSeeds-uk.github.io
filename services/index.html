Our Services

Simple, calm digital-safety support designed for real UK families.

The Digital MOT

The Digital MOT is our most popular service — a guided, judgement-free review of your home’s digital safety. No technical jargon. No scare tactics. Just clear, practical support.

  • Wi-Fi & router security review
  • Device safety checks (phones, tablets, laptops, consoles)
  • Privacy settings & digital exposure review
  • Email & data-leak scan
  • Scam and fraud protection guidance
  • Children’s digital-wellbeing support

You’ll leave with a short, clear list of actions you can take the same day — plus follow-up guidance if needed.

Wi-Fi & Router Safety

Your Wi-Fi is the front door to your digital home. Many households are unknowingly left exposed due to default settings or outdated configurations.

  • Password strength and common risks
  • Security mode (WPA2 / WPA3)
  • Router firmware and setup
  • Guest network configuration
  • Connected and smart devices

We explain what’s safe, what needs attention, and how to protect your home properly — step by step.

Device Security Review

Every device in your household plays a role in digital safety.

  • Out-of-date apps or operating systems
  • Unsafe or overlooked settings
  • App permissions and privacy leaks
  • Console chat, gaming, and social features
  • Built-in security tools that aren’t enabled

We provide clear, non-technical guidance tailored to your family’s devices.

Email & Password Exposure

Many people don’t realise their details have already appeared in past data breaches.

  • Exposure in known breaches
  • Password reuse and weaknesses
  • Risk of account takeover or impersonation

If issues are found, we guide you through fixing them safely and quickly.

Scam & Fraud Awareness

Scams targeting UK families change constantly — from fake delivery messages to impersonation calls and online traps.

  • Red flags in texts, emails, and calls
  • Fake websites and login pages
  • Social-media impersonation
  • Subscription and gaming-related scams

We focus on practical recognition skills that work in real life.

Children’s Digital Wellbeing

Children don’t need fear-based rules. They need understanding, confidence, and support.

  • Safer settings for popular apps
  • Healthy screen-time habits
  • Online friendships and gaming chat
  • Handling pressure, trends, and challenges
  • Building resilience without panic

Our approach is calm, human, and grounded in real family experience.
