What to include

  • Your main question: what you would most like help understanding.
  • Your household context: for example children, shared devices, privacy concerns, or repeated scam messages.
  • Your preferred next step: a simple answer, snapshot guidance, or an audit conversation.

A note on privacy and care

Initial contact is made by email. Please avoid including highly sensitive personal information, passwords, banking details, or private codes in your first message. If more detail is needed, that can be handled more carefully once contact has been established.
