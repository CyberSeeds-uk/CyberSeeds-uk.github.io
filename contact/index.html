Contact Cyber Seeds

Reach out for calm, friendly cyber-safety support

How to Reach Us

Email: cyberseeds.uk@gmail.com

Location: Greater Manchester, UK • Supporting families nationwide

You can email any time with questions about:

  • Booking a home cyber-safety audit
  • Helping children stay safer online
  • Concerns about scams, leaks or hacked accounts
  • Support for vulnerable or elderly family members

What to Include in Your Message

  • Rough number of people and devices in your home
  • Any urgent issues (recent scam, leak, or suspicious activity)
  • Preferred days/times for sessions
  • Whether you’d like a remote or local (Manchester-area) session

This helps us suggest the right service and get you booked in faster.
