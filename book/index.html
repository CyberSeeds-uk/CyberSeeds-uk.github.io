Book Your Home Cyber Audit

A calm, one-to-one session for households who want practical guidance, clearer next steps, and a safer digital home.

During the audit, we look across the Cyber Seeds five-lens model: your home network, devices, privacy settings, scam exposure, and children’s digital wellbeing.

The aim is not blame or technical overload. The aim is to understand your household’s digital environment and build a realistic plan that feels supportive, clear, and useful.

Take the Household Snapshot First

What to Expect

  • Calm assessment: A supportive review of your household’s current digital setup.
  • Clear explanation: Plain-language guidance without shame or unnecessary jargon.
  • Five-lens review: Wi-Fi, devices, privacy, scams, and children’s digital wellbeing.
  • Practical next steps: Simple actions tailored to your home, routines, and priorities.

Before You Book

If you have already completed the Household Snapshot, you can mention that in your email and include anything you would like help with first.

If you have not completed it yet, you can still book directly — but the snapshot is often a useful first step because it helps establish a simple baseline.

A Note on Privacy

Initial contact is made by email. Please avoid sending highly sensitive personal information in your first message. We can discuss anything more detailed safely once contact has been established.
