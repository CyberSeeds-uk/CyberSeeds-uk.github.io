How this works

  1. You complete a short request using the form above.
  2. Your email app opens with a pre-filled message to Cyber Seeds.
  3. You can send it immediately or edit the wording first.
  4. If a saved Household Signal exists on this device, it can be included as helpful context.
  5. Cyber Seeds then responds with the next practical step for your household.

A calm starting point

This is not about blame, panic, or technical pressure. It is about understanding how your household is working today, where strain may be building, and what small, steady changes can improve safety, confidence, and digital wellbeing over time.

Household Snapshot responses remain local to this device unless you choose to include them in your own email request.
