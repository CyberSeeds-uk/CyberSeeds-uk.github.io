Device Hygiene Checklist

Keep phones, tablets and laptops safe with quick, regular checks.

1. Update Your Devices Weekly

Updates patch security holes and protect against malware.

2. Remove Unused Apps

Old apps can have vulnerabilities or risky permissions.

3. Use Strong, Unique Passwords

Combine a password manager with biometric unlock where possible.

4. Enable Automatic Backups

Protect photos, messages and data from loss or device damage.

5. Check App Permissions

Disable microphone, camera, or location access if not required.
