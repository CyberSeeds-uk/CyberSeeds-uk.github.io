Device Hygiene Guide

Keep your phones, tablets, laptops and smart devices safe

Why Device Hygiene Matters

Most cyber-attacks begin with an insecure or outdated device. Good device hygiene protects your family from:

  • ✔ Malware & viruses
  • ✔ Phishing & account takeovers
  • ✔ Data theft
  • ✔ Privacy exposure
  • ✔ Unwanted tracking

Updating settings only takes a few minutes — and makes a massive difference.

Essential Safety Checks

  • ✔ Keep devices updated (software & apps)
  • ✔ Remove unused apps regularly
  • ✔ Enable automatic updates
  • ✔ Turn on device encryption
  • ✔ Use strong PINs & biometric locks
  • ✔ Avoid third-party app stores

Protect Your Accounts

  • ✔ Turn on 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication)
  • ✔ Use unique passwords per device/app
  • ✔ Avoid using Facebook/Google login where possible
  • ✔ Check for password leaks using a trusted tool
