Children’s Risk Index

Understand digital risk levels based on age, habits & online behaviour.

Ages 5–9

High risk from exposure to inappropriate content, unsafe ads, and unmoderated games.

Ages 10–13

Increased risk from social media pressure, online bullying and stranger interactions.

Ages 14–16

Risk transitions to privacy leaks, identity exposure and online persuasion.

Key Protective Steps

  • Use device-level parental controls
  • Review privacy settings together
  • Discuss boundaries around strangers online
  • Monitor new apps and messages
