Children’s Online Safety

Practical tips to help UK parents protect their children online.

1. Set Age-Appropriate Privacy Controls

Limit who can message, friend, and view your child's content on social media.

2. Enable SafeSearch & Content Filters

Activate filtering tools on Google, YouTube, TikTok, and gaming consoles.

3. Talk About Risks Regularly

Normalize conversations around digital safety, strangers, and online pressure.

4. Use Screen Time Boundaries

Balance entertainment with wellbeing through OS-level parental controls.

5. Monitor New Apps & Games

Review age ratings, permissions, and community behaviour before allowing new apps.
