Scam & Phishing Guide

How to spot, avoid and block modern online scams.

1. Check the Sender

Scammers often impersonate banks, HMRC, Royal Mail, NHS or Amazon.

2. Never Click Suspicious Links

Look for misspellings, strange domains, or urgent scare tactics.

3. Verify Before Paying

Call the company using an official number — never the one sent in the message.

4. Use Multi-Factor Authentication

Even if your password leaks, MFA blocks attackers.

5. Report Scams Quickly

Forward texts to 7726, report emails to report@phishing.gov.uk.
