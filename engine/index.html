How to embed

To incorporate the Cyber Seeds assessment engine into your own website or intranet, include our module and custom element. The engine self‑loads the question bank, scoring rules and seed library from the generated directory and stores nothing on our servers. 

<script type="module" src="https://cyberseeds.co.uk/cyberseeds-engine.js"></script>
<cyber-seeds-engine></cyber-seeds-engine>

The component is self‑contained: it automatically fetches the latest snapshot questions, scoring logic and digital seeds. Because it runs entirely in the browser, no data is ever transmitted.

Audit ready by design

Transparency is essential for trust. After completing an assessment, the engine provides a JSON structure containing your answers and all computed metrics. This allows practitioners, researchers and commissioning bodies to verify outcomes against the published scoring model.
