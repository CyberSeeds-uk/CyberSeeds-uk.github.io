Privacy Policy

A clear, friendly explanation of how we protect your data and respect your family’s privacy.

1. Our Promise to You

Cyber Seeds was created to help families feel safer online — and your privacy is a huge part of that. We only collect the information we genuinely need to deliver your Digital MOT, and we never sell, share, or misuse your data. Ever.

2. What Information We Collect

We only collect the information required to provide your session, improve safety advice, and communicate with you:

  • ✔ Your name and contact email
  • ✔ Details you choose to provide in the booking form
  • ✔ Information shared during your Digital MOT session (only with your consent)

We do not collect unnecessary technical data, background tracking, or advertising information.

3. How Your Data Is Used

Your information is used for:

  • ✔ Delivering your Digital MOT session
  • ✔ Sending confirmation emails and follow-up guidance
  • ✔ Improving the quality of our safety recommendations

We do not use your data for marketing unless you explicitly choose to subscribe.

4. How Your Data Is Stored

Your information is stored securely in trusted services such as Outlook and Calendly. Access is restricted and protected with multi-factor authentication.

Any notes made during your session are stored safely and never shared with anyone outside Cyber Seeds.

5. Your Rights

You’re always in full control of your information. You can:

  • ✔ Ask for a copy of the information we hold
  • ✔ Request corrections to any details
  • ✔ Ask us to delete your data at any time
  • ✔ Withdraw consent for communication

To exercise any of these rights, just contact us at cyberseeds.uk@outlook.com.

6. Children’s Privacy

Cyber Seeds is designed to help parents protect their children — not to collect children’s data. We do not gather personal information directly from children at any point.

7. Third-Party Services

We use a few trusted tools to run the service (Calendly, Outlook, Stripe). These services follow strong security standards and only process the minimum required information.

We do not share, sell, or pass your data to advertisers or external companies.

8. How to Contact Us

If you ever have concerns or questions about privacy, please reach out. We take your digital safety seriously — that’s the whole point of Cyber Seeds.

Email: cyberseeds.uk@gmail.com
