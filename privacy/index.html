Privacy Policy

How Cyber Seeds handles your information

1. Who We Are

Cyber Seeds provides home cyber-safety and digital-resilience support to families in Manchester and across the UK. You can contact us at cyberseeds.uk@gmail.com.

2. What Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Your name and contact details (such as email address)
  • Basic information about your household and devices
  • Notes from your session that help us create your action plan

We do not deliberately collect unnecessary personal data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver your home cyber-safety session
  • To create and send your personalised action plan
  • To respond to your questions and follow-up requests

We do not sell your information or use it for unrelated marketing.

4. Security & Confidentiality

We treat your home and digital life with respect. We avoid recording highly sensitive details in writing where possible, and we do not ask for your passwords. Where we need to discuss specific accounts, we encourage you to change details yourself while we guide you.

5. Your Choices

You can:

  • Ask what information we hold about you
  • Request corrections if anything is inaccurate
  • Request that we delete non-essential notes or contact details

To use any of these rights, email us at cyberseeds.uk@gmail.com.
