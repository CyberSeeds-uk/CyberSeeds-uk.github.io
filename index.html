The Five-Lens Safety Model

Your household is a living digital ecosystem. Each lens protects a different part of it.

Network & Wi-Fi
How your internet is configured, shared, and protected — including routers, passwords, and guest access.
Devices & Apps
Phones, tablets, laptops, TVs, consoles — and the apps quietly running on them.
Privacy & Identity
Email accounts, data sharing, old logins, and how much of your household is visible online.
Scams & Behaviour
How messages, calls, and pressure tactics reach your household — and how decisions are made in the moment.
Children’s Wellbeing
How children experience the digital world — not just safety tools, but balance, confidence, and support.
