The Modern Digital Home

Today’s home is a connected environment where family life, learning, communication, and services all meet in one place.

It is normal to feel overwhelmed by changing settings, devices, and expectations.

Every family deserves clear support that builds confidence without blame.

The Five-Lens Safety Model

  • Network & Wi-Fi Safety: Helps households keep home internet access dependable and protected for everyday use.
  • Device Hygiene: Supports simple routines that keep phones, tablets, and computers up to date and working well.
  • Privacy & Identity: Encourages clear account habits that protect personal information and reduce avoidable exposure.
  • Scam Awareness: Builds confidence in spotting suspicious messages, calls, and requests before responding.
  • Children’s Digital Wellbeing: Promotes age-appropriate boundaries and conversations that support children online.

What the Snapshot Is

The Household Snapshot runs locally in your browser, so your responses stay on this device.

It provides a supportive signal to help you decide where to focus first.

It is not a diagnosis, and it is not a compliance test.
