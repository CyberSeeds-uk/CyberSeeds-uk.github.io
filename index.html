About Cyber Seeds

A Domestic Cyber Ecology

Cyber Seeds treats the home as a living system: Wi-Fi, devices, accounts, habits, and wellbeing all interact. Instead of fear, we build care.

The method is designed to be replicable: families can self-check, practitioners can audit, and institutions can adopt consistent standards.

Safeguarding-aware by design

The tone is trauma-aware and non-judgemental. The objective is always the same: reduce harm, restore dignity, and support safer digital life for children and adults.

If you believe a child is at immediate risk, contact emergency services. For non-emergency safeguarding concerns, follow your local authority or school safeguarding route.
