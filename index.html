Why Cyber Defence Matters at Home

Modern UK households rely on Wi-Fi, smartphones, tablets, smart TVs, consoles, cloud accounts and online services every day. But most families aren’t aware of how vulnerable these systems can be — or how quickly small digital gaps can be exploited.

Cyber Seeds gives you friendly, practical guidance to strengthen your home, protect your privacy, reduce exposure, and keep your children safe online. No judgement. No jargon. Just clear steps that work.
