Digital life at home can feel like a lot

Home is now where devices, accounts, school, family life, messages, payments, and online routines all meet.

It is common to feel unsure about what matters most, what can wait, and where to start.

Cyber Seeds is designed to make that picture easier to understand, one step at a time.

The five areas we look at

  • Home Wi-Fi and network safety: helping you keep your home connection stable, private, and safer to use.
  • Device care: supporting simple routines for phones, tablets, laptops, and shared household devices.
  • Privacy and accounts: helping you protect personal information and feel more confident about sign-ins and settings.
  • Scam awareness: building confidence in spotting suspicious calls, messages, links, and requests.
  • Children’s digital wellbeing: supporting age-appropriate boundaries, conversations, and safer online habits.

What the Household Snapshot does

The Household Snapshot is a short, supportive check-in that helps you see where things feel settled and where a small next step may help most.

It runs locally in your browser, so your answers stay on this device unless you choose to export or email them.

It is not a diagnosis. It is not a test. It is a calm way to get your bearings.
