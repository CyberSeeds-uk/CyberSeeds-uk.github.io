Why Cyber Seeds exists

Modern households are ecosystems: routers, devices, accounts, apps, behaviours, and children’s digital lives. Most families never get a map — just scattered tips.

Clarity

Understand your home’s digital shape without fear language.

Proportional safety

Small actions with the biggest protective effect first.

Trauma-aware

Supportive tone. No blaming. No “you’ve failed”.

The Five Lenses

Each lens is a different part of your household digital ecology.

Network & Wi-Fi

Router settings, passwords, guest networks, updates, and who can join your home network.

Device Hygiene

Updates, screen locks, backups, app permissions, and whether devices are maintained or drifting.

Privacy & Exposure

Accounts, data sharing, public profiles, leaked details, and how “findable” the household is.

Scams & Behavioural Risk

Phishing, dodgy links, impulse clicks, parcel scams, fake support calls, and patterns that get exploited.

Child Digital Wellbeing

Age-appropriate boundaries, social apps, contact risks, content exposure, and calm family agreements.
