Key Concepts & Metaphors

Your Router as a Garden Gate

Just like a garden gate keeps intruders out, a secure Wi-Fi router protects your digital home. Tip: Change default router passwords to lock your “gate” – a simple step that keeps your family safe.

Scams – The Mimic Octopus

Scams often disguise themselves as something familiar – much like a mimic octopus blending in. Always double-check unexpected messages or offers. If it feels too good (or too urgent) to be true, it might be an impostor!

Planting Digital Seeds

Small actions grow into strong habits. Every time you reset a password or talk to your child about online life, you’re planting a digital seed. With care and consistency, these seeds bloom into lasting digital wellbeing.

Fun fact: If you’ve ever tried to reset the Wi-Fi by thumping the router... you’re not alone! We’ve all been there – technology can be frustrating. Cyber Seeds is here to help, no judgement, no jargon.
