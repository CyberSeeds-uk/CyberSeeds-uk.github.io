Designed for families

We measure systems (Wi-Fi, devices, habits) — not worth. If things are messy, that’s normal. We start with the smallest move that changes the whole system.

Why “signal”, not “score”

Most digital-safety tools punish people with numbers. Cyber Seeds does the opposite: it shows the shape of your household system — where risk flows, where it’s contained, and what to change first.

System, not blame

We treat the household as an ecosystem: Wi-Fi, devices, accounts, routines, and wellbeing.

Small moves win

We recommend the smallest change that reduces the most risk — without adding stress.

Trauma-aware by default

No judgement language. No “you failed”. Just clear, supportive steps.

The Five Lenses

Cyber Seeds reads your household through five practical safety lenses.

Network

Router hygiene, guest networks, update discipline.

Devices

Updates, locks, backups, shared devices, risky apps.

Privacy

Accounts, exposure, oversharing, recovery controls.

Scam Defence

Urgency messages, verification habits, money risk.

Child Wellbeing

Boundaries, sleep, conflict, calm online routines.
