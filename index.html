The five lenses (the whole household picture)

Most advice only covers one corner (apps, passwords, or screen time). Cyber Seeds maps the entire ecology:

①

Network & Wi-Fi

How your home connects — router habits, guest access, and whether unknown devices can quietly join.

②

Device Health

Updates, lock screens, backups, shared devices, and the little gaps that create big stress later.

③

Privacy & Exposure

What information about your family is public, searchable, or easily misused — often without you realising.

④

Scams & Pressure

How manipulation actually works — urgency, shame, threats, “helpful” messages — and how to break the spell.

⑤

Children’s Digital Wellbeing

Not just screen time: social dynamics, contact risk, algorithms, sleep, mood, and family agreements.
