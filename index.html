Why “signal”, not “score”

Most digital-safety tools punish people with numbers. Cyber Seeds does the opposite: it shows you the shape of your household system — where the risk flows, where it’s contained, and what to change first.

System, not blame

We treat the household like an ecosystem — habits, devices, Wi-Fi, privacy, and wellbeing.

Proportionate actions

We recommend the smallest move that changes the system in your favour.

Trauma-aware by default

No judgement language. No “you failed”. Just clear, supportive steps.

The Five Lenses

Cyber Seeds reads your household through five practical safety lenses.

Network

Wi-Fi setup, router hygiene, guest networks, update discipline.

Devices

Updates, screen locks, backups, shared devices, risky apps.

Privacy

Exposure, oversharing, account protection, data leakage.

Scams

Phishing readiness, money risk, recovery habits, confidence.

Wellbeing

Boundaries, sleep disruption, conflict, kids’ digital safety routines.
