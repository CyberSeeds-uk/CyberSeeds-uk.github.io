Most families are doing their best.

But homes have quietly become digital ecosystems.

Wi-Fi that everything depends on.
Phones that never really switch off.
Accounts, apps, and passwords spread across the household.

A child watching videos upstairs.
A tablet that won’t update anymore.
A message that looks official — but isn’t.

Parents are expected to manage it all, without training, language, or support.

Cyber Seeds exists to change that.

Cyber Seeds is a household digital health check.

It looks at the environment around your family — not individual behaviour.

  • The Wi-Fi your home relies on — how it’s shared and secured
  • Devices in the house — including older phones or tablets still in use
  • Everyday digital habits — reused passwords, shared logins, forgotten accounts
  • Messages and pressure entering the home — scam texts, fake emails, worrying alerts

No surveillance. No monitoring children. No technical jargon.
