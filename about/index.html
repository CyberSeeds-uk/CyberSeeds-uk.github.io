About Cyber Seeds

Cyber Seeds is the UK’s first family-centric digital safety and home cyber-defence service. We help households protect their Wi-Fi, secure their devices, reduce digital risks, prevent scams and guide children safely through online life.

Our mission is simple: to give real UK families the same level of cyber protection that modern businesses rely on — but without complexity, jargon or judgement. You don’t need to understand cybersecurity. You just need clear steps and a trusted advisor.

We bring together cybersecurity expertise, psychology, child-safety awareness and practical guidance to create a safer everyday digital experience for everyone in your home.
