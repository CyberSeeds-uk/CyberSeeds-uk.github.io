About Cyber Seeds

Helping Families Build Safer Digital Lives

Our Mission

Cyber Seeds was created to help families understand digital safety without the stress or confusion. With more online threats than ever — scams, unsafe WiFi setups, weak device security and risks to children — most homes simply don’t know where to start.

Our goal is to make cybersecurity friendly, simple and human. No jargon. No scare tactics. Just calm, practical help.

Who We Help

We work with families across the UK who want clearer guidance on:

  • ✔ WiFi & Router Safety
  • ✔ Device Hygiene
  • ✔ Password & Account Protection
  • ✔ Scam & Fraud Awareness
  • ✔ Children’s Online Safety
  • ✔ Privacy & Data Exposure

Why We Exist

The online world is changing fast — faster than most homes can keep up.

Cyber Seeds offers an easy, guided “Digital MOT” that helps you understand the risks in your home and fix them step-by-step during the session.

Our approach is empowering, not overwhelming — built for real families, not IT professionals.

What Makes Us Different

  • ✔ Family-friendly approach
  • ✔ Clear, calm guidance
  • ✔ Live fixes during the session
  • ✔ Tailored advice for kids, parents & teens
  • ✔ Affordable pricing and simple steps
