Household Snapshot

The Cyber Seeds Household Snapshot

A calm, structured check-in across five lenses of your household’s digital life.

The snapshot is designed to help families understand where things feel settled, where small gaps may be building pressure, and where a realistic next step could help most.

It is supportive rather than judgemental. It is not a diagnosis, and it is not a compliance test.

View Resources

No account. No tracking. Nothing uploaded unless you choose to export or email your results.

What you receive

  • Clear household signal: A simple reading of your current digital position.
  • Priority focus lens: The area most likely to benefit from attention first.
  • Immediate digital seeds: Calm next-step actions for today, this week, and this month.
  • Local-first privacy: Your responses stay on this device unless you choose otherwise.

The Five Lenses

  • Network & Wi-Fi Safety: Home internet access, router habits, and connected-device awareness.
  • Device Hygiene: Updates, everyday routines, and safer defaults across phones, tablets, and computers.
  • Privacy & Identity: Accounts, sign-in habits, recovery settings, and personal information exposure.
  • Scam Awareness: Confidence in recognising suspicious requests, urgency tactics, and message-based threats.
  • Children’s Digital Wellbeing: Household boundaries, conversations, and age-appropriate support online.

Privacy and reassurance

The Household Snapshot runs locally in your browser. That means your responses are processed on this device.

You remain in control of what happens next. You can review your result, save a baseline, export a report, or choose to email your results when seeking support.
